## Description

We are Pixelnest. We craft games and apps.


## History

Pixelnest Studio is Damien Mayance and Matthieu Oger. The company was founded in 2014 in Rennes, France.


## Projects

- [BulletML For Unity](https://pixelnest.io/presskit/bulletml-for-unity/)
- [Shmupfest](https://pixelnest.io/presskit/shmupfest/)
- [Steredenn](https://pixelnest.io/presskit/steredenn/)
- [Steredenn: Binary Stars](https://pixelnest.io/presskit/steredenn-binary-stars/)
